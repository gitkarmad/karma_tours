Extended Stay

Stay longer and enjoy more with our extended stay options.

    Dewachen Hotel & Spa in Phobjikha Valley offers comfortable rooms with traditional decor, a spa, and a restaurant. It's ideal for extended stays with access to nature trails and Gangtey Monastery.

    Osel Thimphu Bhutan offers spacious suites, a fitness center, and a restaurant. Located in central Thimphu, it's perfect for exploring the city's markets, museums, and monasteries.
