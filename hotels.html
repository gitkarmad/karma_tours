Hotels

Explore our selection of hotels, offering luxury and comfort for your stay.

  • Amankora Thimphu Lodge

    Amankora Thimphu Lodge offers a serene retreat in the forests of Thimphu Valley. With 16 suites featuring wood-panelled interiors, a spa, traditional hot stone bath, and a restaurant, it blends Bhutanese architecture with modern luxury. It's an ideal base for exploring Thimphu's cultural landmarks.

  • Taj Tashi Bhutan

    Taj Tashi Bhutan, located in Thimphu's heart, combines Bhutanese heritage with modern comforts. The hotel has 66 rooms adorned with traditional motifs, multiple dining options, a spa, and an indoor pool. Its central location is perfect for exploring Thimphu's markets, monasteries, and cultural sites.
