Motels

Find budget-friendly motels for a comfortable and affordable stay.

  • Wangchuk Lodge

    Wangchuk Lodge in Phobjikha Valley offers cozy, affordable rooms with traditional decor and mountain views. Guests can enjoy local cuisine at the on-site restaurant and visit nearby attractions like Gangtey Monastery and the Black-Necked Crane Center.

  • Bhutan Centennial Lodge

    Located in Paro, Bhutan Centennial Lodge provides budget-friendly accommodations with well-maintained rooms and complimentary breakfast. Its central location is ideal for exploring attractions like Tiger's Nest Monastery and the National Museum of Bhutan.
